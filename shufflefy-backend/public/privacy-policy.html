Privacy Policy for Shufflefy

Effective Date: 13/05/2025

At Shufflefy, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and store your data when you use our website and interact with Spotify's services through our integration.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we request the following Spotify user data through OAuth authentication:

  • User ID: A unique identifier associated with your Spotify account.
  • Playlist IDs: Identifiers for the playlists you create or follow on Spotify.
  • Playback State: Information about the currently playing track, including its progress, which we use to allow you to play, pause, seek, or change tracks.
  • Playback History: The songs you’ve listened to, which we use to tailor your experience (e.g., increasing the weight of songs that haven’t been played in a while).

We request access to the following Spotify scopes to enable these features:

  • user-read-playback-state
  • user-read-currently-playing
  • streaming
  • playlist-read-private
  • user-modify-playback-state

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data collected from your Spotify account for the following purposes:

  • Playback Control: To allow you to control playback (e.g., play, pause, skip, seek, etc.) directly from our website.
  • Playlist Management: To access and display your playlists so that you can interact with them on our platform.
  • Song Weighting: We track the songs you have played to help us increase the weight of songs that haven’t been played recently, improving your experience by offering recommendations based on your listening history.

3. Data Retention and Storage

We store the following information:

  • User ID: We store your Spotify User ID to personalize your experience.
  • Playlist IDs: We store the Playlist IDs to remember which playlists you have interacted with.

This data is stored securely and only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services. If you decide to delete your account or revoke access to Spotify, we will delete all stored data associated with your Spotify account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to provide the services outlined in this policy. Specifically, your data is only shared with Spotify in order to operate the playback features of our website.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and employ appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. User Control and Consent

You have control over your Spotify account and can revoke access to your account at any time by visiting your Spotify Account Settings. If you wish to delete your stored data with us, you can contact us at blakewpframpton@gmail.com, and we will promptly remove your data from our records.

7. Email Communication

We will never email you unless you have explicitly consented to receive communications from us. If you have subscribed to a newsletter or provided consent to receive updates, you can unsubscribe at any time through the email preferences.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, with the updated date indicated at the top. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
